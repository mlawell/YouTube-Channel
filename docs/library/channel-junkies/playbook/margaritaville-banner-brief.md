# Design Brief — Latitude Margaritaville Watersound Channel Banner

**For:** the designer modifying Karen's existing YouTube banner.
**Goal:** adapt the current "Living in Panama City Beach" banner into a matching version for the new **"Living in Latitude Margaritaville Watersound"** channel. Keep the same layout, brand style, fonts, and photo treatment — change only the location-specific copy and background imagery so the two channels feel like one brand.

---

## Source files
- **Editable template:** [`../resources/YT - Channel Banner Templates.pdf`](../resources/YT%20-%20Channel%20Banner%20Templates.pdf) (Channel Junkies Canva banner template).
- **Current live banner (reference):** PCB channel → YouTube Studio → **Customization → Branding → Banner image** (download the current PNG), or view at youtube.com/channel/UCKTGNXomdGLpNjxkrVWlkpw.

## Current PCB banner — what it contains (reference)
- **Headline:** "LIVING IN Panama City Beach, FL"
- **Subhead:** "MOVING? I'M HERE TO HELP!"
- **Contact:** "TEXT/CALL: (850) 517-8528" + "Email: Karen@nwflbeachhomes.com"
- **Karen's photo** on the right (pink top), **beach/Gulf background**, plus **Subscribe + bell** graphics.

---

## Changes for the Margaritaville version
1. **Headline:** `LIVING IN Latitude Margaritaville Watersound` (add `, FL` if it fits the safe area). Keep the same font/styling as the PCB headline.
2. **Subhead:** keep `MOVING? I'M HERE TO HELP!` — or tailor to the niche: `THINKING ABOUT 55+ LIVING? I'M HERE TO HELP!`
3. **Background:** swap the generic Gulf beach shot for **Latitude Margaritaville Watersound imagery** — the community's signature tropical/pastel Margaritaville look: palm trees, the **band shell / town center**, resort pools, or the Watersound coastal vibe. Keep it bright and tropical.
4. **Keep identical** for brand consistency: Karen's photo, the Subscribe + bell graphics, overall layout, fonts, and color treatment.
5. **Contact — must match:** phone `(850) 517-8528` and primary email **`Karen@nwflbeachhomes.com`** (do NOT use countsrealestate.com).
6. *(Optional)* small tag like `55+ Active Adult Community` and a `New videos every week` line.

---

## Specs (YouTube channel banner)
- **Full image:** **2048 × 1152 px** (16:9). Max **6 MB**. PNG or JPG.
- **Safe area (visible on ALL devices):** center **1235 × 338 px** — keep **all text + Karen's face inside this**. Anything outside is cropped on phones.
- Wider/edge areas only show on desktop/TV — use for background imagery, not critical text.

## Deliverables
- `2048 × 1152` banner PNG for the Latitude Margaritaville Watersound channel.
- *(Nice to have)* matching profile picture treatment and a thumbnail style so the new channel is visually consistent with the PCB channel.
