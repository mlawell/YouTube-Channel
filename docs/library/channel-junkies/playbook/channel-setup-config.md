## Part 1 — The standard config (reusable template)

Use this for **every** new channel. Replace `[CITY]`, `[EMAIL]`, etc.

| # | Setting | Where (desktop Studio) | Standard value / rule | Ref |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Account** | youtube.com → Create channel | New **Brand Account** under a **personal Gmail you own forever** — never a brokerage email | Day 1 |
| 2 | **Channel name** | Customization → Basic info | `Living in [Largest Metro] [ST] \| [Team Name]` — biggest metro, not a suburb; include "Team" | Day 1, Ch 8 |
| 3 | **Handle** | Customization → Basic info | Clean `@LivingIn[City][ST]` — no auto-generated truncation | Day 1 |
| 4 | **Profile picture** | Customization → Branding | Professional headshot | Day 2 |
| 5 | **Banner** | Customization → Branding | Face + "LIVING IN [City], [ST]" + "New videos every week" + phone; keep text in the mobile-safe center | Day 2 |
| 6 | **Watermark** | Customization → Branding | Subscribe watermark, full video | Day 3 |
| 7 | **About description** | Customization → Basic info | Relocation-framed, first person, schools/neighborhoods/market/lifestyle, "moving in 9 days or 90", CTA + contact | Ch 7, Day 3 |
| 8 | **Links** | Customization → Basic info | Scheduling link + website; consistent contact | Day 3 |
| 9 | **Keywords** | ⚙ Settings → Channel → Basic info | 10–15 **LOCAL** terms only ("living in [city]", suburbs, "pros and cons [city]"). **Never** national "real estate / real estate agent" | Day 3 |
| 10 | **Category / Language** | ⚙ Settings → Channel → Advanced | People & Blogs; English; License = **Standard YouTube License** | Day 3 |
| 11 | **Feature eligibility** | ⚙ Settings → Channel → Feature eligibility | **Verify phone** → unlocks custom thumbnails + 15‑min+ videos | Day 3 |
| 12 | **Upload defaults** | ⚙ Settings → Upload defaults | Paste templated keyword-rich description + tags so every upload is pre-filled | Day 3, Day 7 |
| 13 | **Layout** | Customization → Layout | Channel trailer (the relocation intro) + featured sections / neighborhood playlists | Day 14 |
| 14 | **Video title** | video → Details | Answer a search question; include city ("Pros & Cons of Living in [City] 2026") | Ch 13, Day 7 |
| 15 | **Thumbnail** | video → Details | Your face + emotion + bold text | Day 7, Day 12 |
| 16 | **Video length** | — | 8–15 min long-form (short-form is not the primary strategy) | Day 4, Ch 18 |
| 17 | **Audience / Altered content** | video → Details | Made for Kids = **No**; Altered/AI = **No** | Day 7 |
| 18 | **End screen / Cards** | video → Editor | End screen (subscribe + next video) in last 15 sec | Day 7 |
| 19 | **Schedule** | upload | Consistent weekly slot (e.g., Thu eve / Sat morning) | Day 7 |
